About Us

Shree Bhairab is a Traditional Musical Band and it is a cultural ensemble renowned for its vibrant performances deeply rooted in traditional Nepali music. The band typically consists of skilled musicians proficient in playing a variety of traditional instruments such as the madal (a type of hand drum), tabla, sarangi (a bowed string instrument), bansuri (bamboo flute), and various percussion instruments. We are an instrumental musical band dedicated to bringing soulful music to life through our performances. Our passion is to connect people through music. 

Our repertoire encompasses a wide range of traditional Nepali folk tunes, bhajans (devotional songs), and classical compositions. Shree Bhairab often performs at cultural events, festivals, weddings, and other celebratory occasions, where their energetic performances captivate audiences and evoke a sense of cultural pride. 

Our music reflects the rich cultural heritage of Nepal, incorporating elements of Hindu and Buddhist traditions, as well as influences from various ethnic communities across the country. Through their music, Shree Bhairab Musical Traditional Band preserves and promotes Nepal's musical heritage, contributing to the preservation of traditional art forms in the modern era.
